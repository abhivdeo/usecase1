Questions to ask the Seekr team
⚙️ Platform & Technology
  • Does SeekrFlow support ingestion of time-series operational data (inverter logs, generation readings) natively, or does it require custom connectors?
  • Can agents be fine-tuned on tabular structured data (Excel/CSV exports from ACMS and ETP), or is fine-tuning limited to text or document formats?
  • How does SeekrFlow handle multi-site data separation — can a single deployment serve multiple plant sites with site-level access control?
📊 Anomaly Detection & Intelligence
  • What anomaly detection models does Seekr offer out of the box? Can thresholds be customised per site or inverter type?
  • Can the platform correlate multiple parameters simultaneously — e.g., linking PR deviation, irradiance, and inverter trips to a probable root cause?
  • How does the system handle sparse or missing data, common in solar O&M logs during grid outages or communication failures?
🚀 Integration & Deployment
  • Is there a REST API or webhook layer to push AI-generated alerts into our existing ACMS mobile app without requiring a full platform rebuild?
  • Does SeekrFlow support Microsoft Fabric or Power BI as a downstream output layer for dashboards and reports?
  • What does a typical integration timeline look like for a two-platform deployment — ACMS first, ETP second?
🗺️ Roadmap & Scalability
  • Can the platform start with rule-based anomaly detection and progressively shift to ML-based prediction as more historical data accumulates?
  • What is Seekr's roadmap for energy and utilities verticals — are there solar or industrial IoT-specific models in development?
  • How does pricing scale as InSolare's plant portfolio grows — per site, per user, or per data volume?
💰 Commercials & Support
  • What is the typical commercial structure — SaaS subscription, usage-based, or a one-time implementation fee plus ongoing support?
  • Is there a pilot or PoC programme available, and what would a 90-day proof of concept on ACMS data look like?
  • What level of ongoing support and model retraining is included — or is that a separate managed services engagement?
🔐 Data Security & Governance
  • Can SeekrFlow be deployed entirely within InSolare's cloud environment (e.g., Azure India region) so plant data never leaves our boundary?
  • How are model outputs made explainable and auditable — particularly for fault attribution that may have contractual implications with clients?
  • What certifications does SeekrFlow carry — ISO 27001, SOC 2, or equivalent?
